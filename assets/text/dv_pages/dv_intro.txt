On January 14, 2025, shortly into her first weeks as Jackson County Prosecuting Attorney, Melesa Johnson delivered a letter to Jackson County law enforcement agencies and municipal prosecuting attorneys, directing them to begin submitting serious domestic violence and drug distribution cases to her office for felony charge consideration. 

On April 7, 2025, Prosecuting Attorney Johnson joined a press conference with KCPD Chief Graves, Kansas City Mayor Lucas, and service providers to bring awareness to the spike in domestic violence-related homicides in Kansas City, MO and the resources available to victims.
